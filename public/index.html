Welcome to SpotifyVoteApp

A simple way to vote for music in your WhatsApp group.

Introduction

Welcome to SpotifyVoteApp! This app allows you to collaboratively vote on music tracks within your WhatsApp group. Here's how you can use the app:

  • Start a New Session: Begin a voting session for a new playlist.
  • Submit a Track: Share your favorite Spotify tracks for voting.
  • Vote on a Track: Cast your vote for tracks shared by others.
  • View Results: See which tracks received the most votes.

Get started by using the forms below to interact with each feature.
